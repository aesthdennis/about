Time is precious, life is precious—and living life efficiently is what I do; therefore, I will recommend only one book from the authors that I consider to have influenced me, and that I think you should read as well.

  • Martin Heidegger: "Being and Time"
  • Albert Camus: "The Myth of Sisyphus"
  • Friedrich Nietzsche: "The Will to Power"
  • Arthur Schopenhauer: "On Women"
  • Jordan Peterson: "Maps of Meaning"
  • Søren Kierkegaard: "Either/Or"
  • Mircea Eliade: "The Sacred and the Profane: The Nature of Religion"
  • Will Durant: "The Story of Philosophy"
  • David Hume: "My Own Life"

Literature
